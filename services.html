The Problems I Solve

I focus on the three strategic challenges I see most often across growth-stage companies:

  • AI to Production
  • Data Silos
  • Team Misalignment
  • Unclear Priorities

“We’re experimenting with AI but can’t get to production”

The Problem

Your data scientists have impressive demos, but enterprise security requirements, infrastructure complexity, and operational concerns prevent deployment. Months pass, budgets grow, and nothing ships to customers.

My Approach

I build MLOps frameworks that let teams deploy and iterate quickly while meeting your compliance requirements. Using AWS Bedrock, containerization, CI/CD pipelines, and production monitoring, I help you ship AI systems in weeks, not months.

Recent result: Delivered production-ready Gen AI system in 10 weeks (vs 6+ months typical), enabling analysts to process financial data 3x faster.

“Our data is everywhere and nowhere”

The Problem

You have valuable data scattered across systems—CRM, operational databases, analytics platforms—but it’s inaccessible when you need it. Customer questions take days to answer instead of minutes. AI initiatives fail because data isn’t ready.

My Approach

I architect unified data platforms that make trapped data available while scaling globally. Using modern data stack tools (Snowflake, Databricks, AWS Glue) and proven architectural patterns, I ensure your data becomes a competitive advantage.

Recent result: Architected data platform serving 500+ customers across 40 countries, dramatically reducing support costs while enabling enterprise expansion.

“Our tech team is brilliant but not aligned to business goals”

The Problem

Your engineers are capable, but sprints don’t translate to business impact. Leadership requests features, engineering builds them, but somehow nothing moves the needle. Technical and business teams speak different languages.

My Approach

I translate between technical and business teams, creating clarity and focus that drives measurable results. I help teams understand business context, prioritize ruthlessly, and measure outcomes—not just activity.

Recent result: Transformed client onboarding by architecting event-based system, reducing onboarding time by 70% and dramatically improving activation rates.

“We need to move faster but don’t know what to prioritize”

The Problem

You’re trying to do everything at once, so nothing ships. Every stakeholder has urgent needs. The backlog has 200+ items. Your team is overwhelmed and burned out.

My Approach

I bring pattern recognition from working across dozens of companies. I help you identify the vital few initiatives that actually move the needle, say no to everything else, and execute with focus. Strategic prioritization isn’t about clever frameworks—it’s about courage and discipline.

Recent result: Helped enterprise software company go from 15 simultaneous initiatives (nothing shipping) to 4 focused initiatives (all delivered on time with high quality).
